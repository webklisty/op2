Payment methods accepted by Rikofranchise

  1. Payment via Mpesa
  2. Debit & Credit Cards
  3. Bank Transfer
  4. Mobile Money

1.1 Payment via Mpesa

You may make payments for your purchases from the Riko marketplaces via Mpesa in checkout by providing your actual phone number in which a prompt will be sent on your phone to enter the mpesa pin to complete your payments.

1.2 Debit & Credit Cards

You may make payments for your purchases from the Riko marketplace by using your debit or credit card.

1.3 Bank Transfer

You may make payments for your purchases from the Riko marketplace by bank transfer. You will be required to input your bank information at the checkout process.

1.4 Mobile Money

You may make payments for your purchases from the Riko marketplace via mobile money transfer. You will be required to input your mobile money account details at the checkout process.

1.5 Promotions and Discounts

Riko may offer various other promotions and discounts that may be applied towards a purchase on a Riko marketplace and which may be subject to specific terms and conditions.
