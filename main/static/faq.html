Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order ?

To place an order, follow these steps:
Browse our Website: Start by exploring our wide range of products. You can use the search bar or browse through categories to find what you need.
Select the Product: Click on the product you wish to purchase to view more details. Ensure that you've chosen the right size, color, and quantity.
Add to Cart: Once you've made your selection, click the 'Add to Cart' or 'Buy Now' button. This action adds the item to your shopping cart.
Review Your Cart: To review your order, click on the shopping cart icon , usually located at the top right of the page. Here, you can adjust the quantity, remove items, or apply more codes.
Proceed to Checkout: When you're ready to place your order, click 'Checkout'. You will be directed to the checkout page.
Shipping Information: Provide your shipping address, ensuring it's accurate and up to date as indicated in your address book in your profile dashboard.
Payment Details: Select your preferred payment method (Lipa na Mpesa, PayPal) and enter the necessary payment details. Don't forget to double-check the information for accuracy.
Review Your Order: Take a moment to review your order, including the products, quantity, shipping address, and payment method. Make sure everything is correct.
Place Your Order: Once you are satisfied with your order details, make payments.' This action confirms your order and initiates the payment process.
Order Confirmation: You will receive an order confirmation on the website, and a confirmation email will be sent to your provided email address. This email will include your order number and a summary of your purchase.
Track Your Order: After placing your order, you can track its status through your account.
That's it! Your order is now placed, and you can look forward to receiving your products within the estimated delivery time. If you have any questions or encounter any issues during the ordering process, feel free to contact our customer support for assistance."
